About ELHAM FM Services

ELHAM FM Services is a Saudi-based Facilities Management company specializing in both Soft Services and Hard Services. We deliver professional, reliable, and high-quality solutions for residential, commercial, and corporate facilities across Saudi Arabia.

Our Mission

To provide cost-effective, professional, and reliable facility management services that enhance cleanliness, safety, and comfort for our clients.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and recognized Facilities Management provider in Saudi Arabia, known for service excellence and long-term client partnerships.

Our Expertise

ELHAM FM Services follows structured operational procedures, strict quality control, and professional workforce management to ensure consistent service delivery. We aim to provide peace of mind to our clients by maintaining their facilities at the highest standards.

Soft Services (Cleaning & Support)

Soft Services focus on cleanliness, hygiene, and support functions for facilities. Our services include:

  • Commercial & Corporate Cleaning
  • Residential & Villa Cleaning
  • Housekeeping & Janitorial Staffing Solutions
  • Hospitality & Support Services
  • Pest Control Services
  • Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance
  • Sofa Upholstery and Carpet Shampooing

Hard Services (Technical & Maintenance)

Hard Services focus on the technical and mechanical aspects of building management. We ensure your property remains fully functional and well-maintained. Our services include:

  • HVAC Installation, Maintenance, and Repair
  • Duct Cleaning and Gas Refilling
  • Electrical Maintenance
  • Plumbing Services
  • Civil Works: Painting, Tiling, Gypsum, Carpentry
  • Preventive & Corrective Maintenance
  • Energy Efficiency Optimization

Our Approach
